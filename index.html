ABOUT CATERING

Tradition since 2014

The Catering was founded in 2014 by Mr. ashish agarwal in dhanji ki gali vidhyadhar nagar jaipur, MITTAL Catering provides top notch service and culinary excellence at thousands of weddings, corporate events and special occasions.

Our delectable food, first class presentation and careful attention to our product and the needs of our clients has resulted in 8 successful years and an excellent reputation among our many happy clients and their guests.

Our goal is not simply to make your event a beautiful and joyful experience, but to craft a unique event that will transcend your every expectation.

MITTAL Caterers understands how to create simply brilliant events through listening, caring & being passionate with a touch of a total fabulousness!
